Gold Macro Correlations
↕ INVERSE — Gold vs USD (DXY)
Strong inverse: when USD strengthens, gold weakens (and vice versa). Dollar-denominated asset dynamic.
↑ POSITIVE — Gold vs Brent Crude
Oil spikes raise inflation fears → investors buy gold as inflation hedge. Energy crises amplify both.
⚡ HORMUZ CRISIS — Feb 28, 2026
Strait closure cut 20% of global oil supply, triggering war risk insurance premiums. Gold responded as the primary safe-haven, surpassing all historical ATH records.
🏛 CENTRAL BANK BUYING
Central banks (China, India, Russia) increased gold reserves throughout 2024-2026, reducing USD dependency and providing sustained upward price pressure.
